Our Story

Friday, September 13, 2019

Just another Friday—or so they thought. The bride and groom were going about their usual lives, completely unaware that a random party with mutual friends was about to change everything.

The groom was intrigued from the start—there was just something about her. The bride, on the other hand, wasn’t quite as easily impressed. It took a little charm, a lot of patience, and possibly some strategic texting. But four months later, they were officially a couple—hopeful, happy, and settling into something that felt surprisingly right.

Five years later, here they are—still figuring it out, still laughing, and apparently ready to sign paperwork about it.
